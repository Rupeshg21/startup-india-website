Tourism and Hospitality is one of the biggest employers forming one of the key pillars of the service sector in the country.

In FY'20, the tourism sector in India accounted for 39 million jobs (8% of total employment)
Despite the recent downturn, the sector will account for about 53 million jobs by 2029

The travel sector is also witnessing a significant change and evolution on account of the pandemic and the constantly changing preferences of tourists. Some of them may include utilizing the cultural heritage and creation of hospitality infrastructure in rural India or utilizing the existing infrastructure for medical tourism and healthcare support during this ongoing pandemic.

Villotale is a tech startup in destination and experiential tourism in rural space engaging the local community. Upcurve Business Services Private Limited

DIPP54647

udChalo aims to simplify life by building unique, convenient, and cost-effective platforms for soldiers to access consumer products and services. udChalo Services, including flights, hotels, and tax filing, are now available to everyone at affordable costs coupled with reliable customer service.

Product Summary

udChalo operates travel services for Defence Personnel through the website www.udchalo.com, app platform, and 70 plus offline ticket booking offices catering to more than 2.8 million serving Defence Personnel, Veterans, and their Dependants. udChalo's booking offices are operated by Veterans/Veer Naris/Dependents from the Indian Armed Forces Community. Besides offering the convenience of Air travel, the company has also introduced Hotels, Taxis, Buses, Trains, and Tax filing as additional service offerings to its customers. It is in the process of integrating more services into its portfolio.
